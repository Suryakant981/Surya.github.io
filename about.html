About Me

I am a Final-Year BCA Student with a deep passion for IT Infrastructure and Software Development. My journey in technology is driven by a desire to create autonomous solutions that solve critical real-world problems. This ambition led to the development of SENTINEL-X, a context-aware 3-tier autonomous SOS system that utilizes 'Dead Man’s Switch' logic to ensure user safety and hardware security without relying on active internet connectivity.

My technical background is comprehensive, featuring a one-year Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA) and proficiency in Tally. I have a strong command over Python and Web Development, skills I further polished during a professional internship at Codec Technologies where I focused on responsive design and technical SEO.

Beyond coding, I have experience in IT Administration and Office Management, allowing me to approach projects with both a technical and an organizational mindset. Whether it’s managing complex databases with SQLite or configuring local execution environments for AI projects, I am committed to building efficient, reliable, and scalable technology.
