Privacy Policy for SENTINEL-X

Last updated: 2026

1. Data Collection & Handling

Our application, SENTINEL-X, accesses local Hardware Sensor APIs (GPS) and Telephony Services (SMS) exclusively to ensure user safety during emergencies. All operations are processed locally on the device.

2. Offline Processing & Security

The core emergency dispatch system operates 100% independent of internet connectivity. No personal data, location telemetry, or contact information is transmitted to external servers or third-party cloud services. Data persists solely within the device's localized TinyDB and SQLite secure caching layers.

3. User Consent

Permissions for Location and SMS monitoring are explicitly requested at runtime. Users maintain absolute control over custom countdown sequences and can terminate background monitoring tasks at any point through the system controls.
